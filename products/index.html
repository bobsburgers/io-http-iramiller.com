Products

Tigo CTM uses Lamassu machines because they are the most popular in the industry, and they have a free and open software stack.

For each level, Tigo operators also get Dash, cosigning, and other value add features pre-installed.

Douro

Lamassu’s basic model, the Douro, lets users buy Bitcoin with cash. This one-directional operation is the simplest place to start, and may have legal advantages in some jurisdictions.

Braga

This floor stand is designed to match the Douro, and cuts down installation work by 90%. Tigo can help with custom installations, for those who desire.

Santa Tirso

Lamassu’s two-way upgrade Santa Tirso, lets users buy and sell Bitcoin with cash. It also doubles as a stand, replacing the Braga.
